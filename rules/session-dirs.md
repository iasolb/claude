# Claude workspace dirs

`~/claude/` is the Claude workspace on both machines (PC set up 2026-07-19,
Mac mirror pending). Alongside the memory-bank repo and myToolBox it holds
`session/` with three folders, each with a distinct job:

- `session/context/`: my drop zone, files I put there for you to look at.
  Check it at the start of a session and whenever I say something is "in
  context".
- `session/working/`: your scratch/working space, and the landing zone for
  machine-sync's `pull.*.claude` commands (see myToolBox).
- `session/outputs/`: deliverables I asked for (reports, exports, generated
  artifacts) land here, not scattered around the filesystem.

Contents are mine to clean up, don't prune these folders unilaterally.
